About Us

Loft conversion specialist and company founder, Kurtis Knightley has developed a customer centric approach to every job his team completes. With their reliable work ethic, friendly attitude and passion for what they do, Kurtis and his team pride themselves on their high standards. The team of skilled Architects, Electricians, Plumbers and Tilers collaborate with one another to ensure that every project Ridgeline Lofts take on is executed to a faultless standard.

Our Approach

At Ridgeline Lofts we work closely with our customers every step of the way. Offering professional advice and taking all of your ideas into account, we strive to make the duration of the build a hassle and stress free experience for you.

We understand getting a loft conversion or having any type of building work done on your home is not only a big commitment, but can be nerve racking to some. Which is why we are always on hand to put your mind at ease, simply give us a call to discuss any questions you may have regarding the build.

Ridgeline Lofts use only the highest quality materials and respected, quality tradesmen who are the best in their fields and have positive reputations. All of the tradesmen we work with are fully insured, giving you complete reassurance and peace of mind.

Ridgeline Lofts will treat your home as if it was our own. At the end of every working day we will clean the site from top to bottom. During the build we will also do our upmost to minimise the impact on your daily life, apart from the general noise from power tools etc, there will be very minimal disruption.

We take great care when working in your home, we take all the necessary precautions to protect your floors, carpets and walls making sure no damage is made. We will keep things tidy and are professional at all times.

No hidden charges. Our pricing structure is clear and easy to understand, every quote is detailed and personal to you and your ideas.

All of our work has a three year guarantee for complete peace of mind.

If you would like to speak to some of our previous customers regarding their build, we can arrange this. We have many existing customers who hold us in high regard, who would be delighted to share their loft conversion experience with you.
